There are three types of model relations
1. One-Many Relation
2. One-One Relation
3. Many-Many Relation



1. One-Many:
   If you have to group the items/entities under certain group then One-Many relationship is maintained
   E.g. Food Category and Food Menu, Classroom and Student,  Height and Person

2. One-One:
    If you have to make one on one relationship between entities then One-One relation is maintained.
    E.g. User and UserProfile

3. Many-Many
    E.g. User and User Role in a system
